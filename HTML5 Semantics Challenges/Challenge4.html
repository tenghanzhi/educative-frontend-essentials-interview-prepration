Algebra Puzzle by MathIsFun.com

Solve the following (each letter is a different digit):

CAT = (C + A + T) × C × A × T

Solution

135 = (1 + 3 + 5) × 1 × 3 × 5

If you allow A=T, then another solution (supplied by Bill Elliott) is:

144 = (1 + 4 + 4) × 1 × 42
